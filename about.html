About Us

‘Unitedmizu Industries Pvt. Ltd.’ (UMI) is a well-established and growing engineering company. Led by the team of professionals, supplying innovative Turnkey Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) Solution based on Caustic Recovery Plants (CRP), Multiple Effect Evaporator Plants (MEE), Agitated Thin Film Dryers (ATFD) & Stripper Systems (SS). Our approach is based on knowledge, concept, design, planning and execution.





‘Unitedmizu Industries Pvt. Ltd.’ has a state-of-art Manufacturing facility. The Quality Assurance is with stage wise inspection. The Manufacturing facility has the capability to work with materials of construction of all grades of steel such as MS, CS, SS 304, SS 316, SS 316L, Duplex. ‘United Mizu India’ has a professional and highly experienced Project Management & Execution team.
